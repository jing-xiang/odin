Beef Stroganoff

Description

This classic beef stroganoff recipe is quick and easy to make, perfect for a weeknight meal. Tender beef strips are cooked with onions and mushrooms, then finished with a creamy sour cream sauce. Serve over egg noodles for a hearty and delicious dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. beef sirloin steak, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 8 oz. mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 1 tbsp. all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Egg noodles, cooked according to package directions
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Season the beef strips with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the beef strips and cook until browned on all sides, about 3-4 minutes. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the onions and mushrooms and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  6. Sprinkle the flour over the vegetables and stir to combine.
  7. Gradually add the beef broth, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  8. Add the Worcestershire sauce and bring the mixture to a simmer.
  9. Add the sour cream and stir until well combined.
  10. Return the beef strips to the skillet and cook until heated through, about 2-3 minutes.
  11. Serve the beef stroganoff over cooked egg noodles.
  12. Garnish with chopped parsley, if desired.
